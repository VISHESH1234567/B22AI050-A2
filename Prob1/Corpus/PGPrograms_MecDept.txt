M.Tech-PhD Dual Degree
The department currently offers an M.Tech-PhD dual degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Advanced Manufacturing and Thermofluids. A wide range of specialization related courses is currently offered.

Specialization: Mechanical Design/ Thermofluids/ Advanced Manufacturing

The department offers M. Tech-PhD dual course in Mechanical Engineering within three major domains of Thermofluids, Mechanical Design, and Smart Manufacturing. The students also have the opportunity to work with an interdisciplinary specialization in emerging areas such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Data and Computational Science, Smart Healthcare, Energy Materials, and Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS).
